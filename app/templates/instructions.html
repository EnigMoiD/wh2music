What is WH2Music?

It's a music player being run on a Raspberry Pi - maybe even in WH2! The Pi plays the music, and you tell it what to play on the the front page.

The Search is Kinda Crappy

We're working on it!

What if it doesn't have my favorite song?

You can upload it! Just make sure that all the metadata is correct before you confirm the upload. The upload button is at the bottom of the front page.

It's too loud!

Press Ctrl+'<' to lower the volume. When you're ready to party, press Ctrl+'>' to raise it again.
